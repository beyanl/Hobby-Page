The Wonderful World of Books

My childhood memories are quite blurred and I don't remember much. I do however, remember always being lost in a book.
Till this day, you can find me "bingeing" on a book, basically sitting for hours in one place lost in a book surrounded by snacks.

My fave genres

  • Romance (so I can stay a hopeful romantic)
  • Dystopia (because reality clearly isn't scary enough)
  • Fantasy (sometimes humans are too much to deal with)
